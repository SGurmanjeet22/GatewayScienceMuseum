Women In STEM

THE ISSUE

The lack of women within STEM Fields.

With the rise of technology within this modern world the need for women within technical fields is even more prevalent. But the discrimination and prejudice that women face within stem fields hinder their ability to be a part of modern innovation. To get rid of barriers like discrimination and prejudice, change must occur on the structural level within society.
